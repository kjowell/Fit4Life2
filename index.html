Life is about the choices that we make. You making the choice to take the first step in the discovery process of healthy living will be one of the most important! Healthy living is not just a diet to reach a goal weight and then think "Phew! I made it! I'm done!" It's the development of new patterns and new habits.

Here's how this works and here's where it all begin: you will step through each of the sections mentioned below and check off all that apply to you - your body, your mind and your sense of well being. Once you've completed all of the sections, hit the submit button to submit this information to a local Shaklee representative.
